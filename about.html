About Us

Discover our story and the people behind MotionSite.

Who We Are

MotionSite started as a small group of designers and developers driven by curiosity. Today, we are a global collective sharing knowledge through interactive media.

Our contributors range from industry veterans with decades of experience to enthusiastic newcomers bringing fresh perspectives.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MotionSite grew out of a need for deeper, more engaging technical content online. We began publishing tutorials infused with animations to maintain reader interest without sacrificing depth.

By 2022, we crossed one million monthly readers and hosted workshops across three continents. Today, we continue to innovate, exploring WebGL, generative art, and beyond.
